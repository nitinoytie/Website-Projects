Advanced Roofing System

Advanced Roofing System is based on latest Polyurethane Technology, keeping the heat out, reducing the use of Airconditioner which can save up to 40% of electricity bills.
Due to its double layered monolithic system & its rigid closed cell construction in the foam, forms an imperiable waterproofing layer ensuring 100% no water leakage. Its a comprehensive roofing system supplied & installed by a single manufacturer, complying to the Thermal Insulation Standards set by Dubai Municipality.

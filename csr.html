Corporate Social Responsibility

Businesses cannot be successful when the society around them fails. We at SUROJ have always ensured making our contribution towards the social surroundings and bring as much change as required to create a better world.

As a part of our CSR activities, we have played our bit by launching 'Save Farmer Save Nation Campaign,' 'Cancer Awareness and Motivational Workshop,' 'Mobile Toilet Facilities for all Varkaris,' 'Crèche' for Small Children of Construction Workers at the site,' 'Tree Plantation Drive; Planting over 410 Samplings across all our sites in India', 'Blood Donation Drive; Raising awareness about how it can help save a million of lives’ and many more.

With every such step, we get a better understanding of our society and its problems. This understanding then serves as a ladder to bringing another positive change in our social surroundings.

Trading

Noble International is one of the leading retail traders of a wide range of building material products. The company is based in Dubai (UAE).It has two distribution outlets and a team of efficient sales executives and staff. Realizing the vital importance of quality management and service, our clients and customers list has been showing sustain development from the very early days of its establishment.

While Noble International in whole range of building material products, its core trading is electrical and plumbing items. The company main stock list is:

  • Electrical Items
  • Sanitary products and fittings
  • Ceramic tiles
  • Hardware
  • Motors and pumps
  • Paints and glues
  • Mixers
  • PVC and GI pipes
